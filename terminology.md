# Terminology Clarification (Reference)

## Recursive Self-Reconstruction
A system capability in which internal structures, models, or processes
are modified or regenerated by the system itself.

## Institutional Responsibility
The ability to clearly identify accountable human or organizational actors
for decisions, approvals, and outcomes.

## Review Rejection
A determination that a system or operation is not eligible for review
without implying prohibition or enforcement.

## Post-Incident Stabilization
Institutional measures intended to prevent escalation, precedent formation,
or responsibility diffusion following an incident.
